About Us

Tradition since 2015

Kay's Cupcake and Pastries, started with a passion for baking and a love for sweet treats. Our journey began in a small kitchen, nestled in the heart of Diego Martin, has been delighting the community with its delectable treats since its founding and today we serve our community with the same dedication and love for baking.


With a deep love for crafting delicious baked goods, this bakery quickly became a local favourite. Kay's commitment to quality and creativity is evident in every bite, whether mouth-watering pastries or exquisitely decorated cupcakes with innovative new flavours to excite the taste buds of regular patrons and newcomers alike. Each creation is a testament to Kay's dedication and the culmination of years of perfecting recipes and baking techniques.
